DAY 02: Leave Varanasi At 05.30 Hrs And Arrive Allahabad Around 08.30 Hrs Sri Sundaram Sastrigal Will Arrange For Sankalpam, Prayachitham, Venidhanam, Sethu Pooja And Theertha Srardham And Also For Triveni Sangamam Snan And After Lunch Visit Anand Bhavan And Leave For Varanasi And Night Stay.

DAY 03: Morning Pancha Katta Theertha Srardham And Homa Srardham In Kasi And After Lunch Visit Viswanathar, Visalakshi And Annapurani Darshan And Ganga Aarthi In The Evening And Night Stay.

DAY 05: Check Out Hotel From 07.00 Hrs And Arrive Gaya(12 Kms) Perform Rituals For Ancestors In Three Places – Palguni River, Vishnu Pad And Home Srardham In Sastrigal House From 08.00hrs To 03.00pm . Leave To Varanasi And In Buddha Gaya Visit Main Temple- Bodhi Tree, 80 Feet Buddha And Night Stay In Varanasi.

DAY 06: Morning Darshan Of Viswanathar, Annapurani And Visalakshi Temple And Kal Bairav Tempe And Leav By Flight To Chennai.

DAY 01: Chennai To Sringaar By Flight On Arrival Proceed To Pahalgam And Overnight Stay.
