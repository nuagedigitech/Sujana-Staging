View Tour Details 

DAY1: CHENNAI TO SRINGAAR BY FLIGHT ON ARRIVAL PROCEED TO PAHALGAM AND OVERNIGHT STAY.

DAY2: AFTER BREAKFAST PAHALAGAM SIGHT SEEING – VISIT CHANDANWARI ENTRY POINT TO AMARNATH YATHRA, BETAAB VALLEY AND ARU VALLEY  AND AFTER LUNCH LEAVE TO SRINAGAR ENROUTE VISIT  CRICKET BAT FACTORY AND SAFFRON FIELDS AND OVERNIGHT STAY IN SRINAGAR.

DAY3:  AFTER BREAKFAST LEAVE FOR GULMARG- ENJOPYR THE CABLE CAR RIDE. FORGET NOT TO MISS IN VISITING THE SECOND PHASE AND ENJOY THE SCEINCE BEAUTY. RETURN TO SRINGAR IN THE EVENING AND OVERNIGHT STAY.

DAY4: LEAVE EARLY MORNING TO SONAMARG AND VISIT TWO OR THREE POINTS AND RETURN TO SRINGAR AND OVERNIGHT STAY.

DAY5: AFTER BREAKFAST VISIT SANKARACHARYA TEMPLE IN THE HILLS WHICH IS HIGHEST POINT IN SRINAGAR, SHALIMAR GARDEN, NISHAT BAGH EVENING BOATING IN DAL LAKE AND OVERNIGHT STAY.

DAY6 : LEAVE SRINGAR AND ARRIVE CHENNAI WITH SWEET MEMORIES.
